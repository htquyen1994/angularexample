WARNING!

Changing this setting will effect the amount of resources your computer dedicates to running Periscope. Dedicating more resources to Periscope will reduce the amount of resources available to other applications on your computer.

In general, the more resources dedicated to Periscope the faster it will run.

In most instances, e.g. when using a typical business laptop or desktop PC, the default setting of 'Medium' is advised.

If you are using an older computer then you might consider moving to the 'Low' setting.

If you are using a modern powerful computer with plenty of RAM then switching to the 'High' setting will dedicate more resources to Periscope and hence the application will typically run faster.
